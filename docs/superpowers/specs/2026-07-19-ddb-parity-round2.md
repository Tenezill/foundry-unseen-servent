# DDB Parity Report — Round 2 (2026-07-19)

Round 1 (M10–M14, `docs/superpowers/plans/2026-07-08-ddb-parity-roadmap.md`) closed the breadth gaps against a level-3 reference wizard; this round compared four live level-12 characters from the "all the curses" campaign (Miterdandes Eisenseele, Kobal Taltos, Corvo, Hahlen "Ard" Ursar) and found the remaining gaps are about **depth, not breadth**. Every sheet area exists in the app, but the combat-critical numbers a player checks mid-turn are incomplete: no spell save DC or spell attack bonus is surfaced anywhere, actions have no Action/Bonus/Reaction economy lanes, and client-computed damage drops riders (Lifedrinker, Sun Blade, Potent Spellcasting) even though the CHA/Hexblade attack-ability override already works. A consistent pattern emerged: the data almost always arrives from Foundry (activation types, versatile dice, spell materials, weapon ranges are all present in captured fixtures) and is discarded by the adapter — so most gaps are charter-safe rendering work, not rules-engine work. Of 20 verified gaps: 3 high (2 missing, 1 partial), 8 medium, 9 low; four of the low-impact ones (level-up management, custom-action authoring, optional-feature gating, sheet decorations) are wholly or largely covered by the existing "companion, not builder" non-goals.

## Suggested next milestones

Numbering continues after the shipped M23 (custom items). All four milestones are adapter + SDK-field + PWA rendering work — no rules engine, consistent with the charter. Gaps #16, #17, #19, #20 stay non-goals (or, for #16, a candidate stretch item in M24 since it is a boolean preference filter, not rules logic).

### M24 — Caster confidence (spellcasting depth) — gaps #1, #8; effort S–M
The highest-value round-2 win and the cheapest: everything is already in the actor data. Emit spell save DC (`8 + prof + mod`) and spell attack (`prof + mod`) as headline/overview stats (the mock server already proves the Stat UI renders a "Spell DC" tile); extract per-activity save DCs (`dc.calculation: spellcasting` is in the fixtures) onto feature and spell rows; enrich the spell sub-line and detail dialog with components (V/S/M), costed materials, casting time, range, and duration — the SDK doc comment (`ListItem.sub`, "3rd level · V,S") already anticipated this shape.

### M25 — Turn economy & weapon fidelity (actions depth) — gaps #2, #3 (rider half), #5, #6, #7; effort M–L
One coherent SDK change unlocks the whole group: add an `activation`/metadata block to `ActionDescriptor` (activation type, attack count, properties, range, versatile die, ammo link). Then: group the Actions tab into Action / Bonus / Reaction / Special lanes (`activation.type` arrives from Foundry today and is dropped); show attacks-per-Attack-action (Extra Attack ×3, Thirsting Blade); render property/range chips on attack rows; add a one-/two-handed damage choice for versatile weapons (`system.damage.versatile` + a `handedness` param on the damage intent); aggregate damage riders — at minimum `system.magicalBonus`, extra activity damage parts, and ability-shaped bonuses like `@abilities.wis.mod` so Lifedrinker/Sun Blade/Potent Spellcasting damage stops being wrong. Ammo auto-decrement can ride the existing `item.<id>.qty` resource. Largest milestone of the four; split the rider aggregation into its own PR.

### M26 — Situational awareness (vitals & inventory polish) — gaps #4, #11, #15, #18; effort M
Add an optional `tags`/`source` affordance to the `Stat` row type, then: restriction-tagged advantage badges on saves/skills ("Adv. vs poison", "Stealth — sound only") sourced from effect data where derivable, pre-selecting the existing manual picker; source attribution on defenses via ActiveEffect `origin` ("Fire — Potion of Fire Resistance"); encumbered/heavily-encumbered tier badge from Foundry's derived `encumbrance.pct/encumbered` (already fetched in `enrich`) plus a coin-weight-aware local fallback; render `attunementDescription` condition text and a soft cap warning at 3/3 (display-only — enforcement stays with Foundry).

### M27 — Identity & lore round 2 (description depth) — gaps #9, #10, #12, #13, #14; effort M
Extends M11 now that real characters show the need. Render the DDB-equivalent notes fields and structured identity rows (alignment, faith, age/height/etc., lifestyle) where the Foundry actor carries them; surface the background name in the hero/Character section and attribute background-granted features ("Wanderer — Outlander") instead of the bare "Feat" label; lift the round-1 deferral on phone editing with a minimal text-write intent (biography, personality one-liners, item name/note — the "Parry Dagger" rename use case), reusing the existing `update-item`/update-actor relay writes and the sanitization pipeline. Live-verify the relay accepts `details.*` text writes before committing to scope.
